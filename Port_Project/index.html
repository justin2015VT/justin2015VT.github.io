What is Port?

Port is a collaborative space that provides innovative technology to assist you in your endeavors. There are three specific zones in addition to the collaborative space. The three zones are listed below:

  • Explore Zone
  • Show and Tell Zone
  • Crunch Zone

Please select the corresponding titled link above to learn more about what each zone has to offer you.

What Can Port Do For You?

Port offers high-end technology that can enhnace your projects to a more professionally marketable level.

Are You In This Alone?

Absolutely not! Port offers workshops to show you how to use specific software and Port has consulting hours in case you are in working within a progam and get stuck. Also available to assist you is the man in charge, Bruce Pencek. As you can see, there is no shortage to the amount of assistance that is at your disposal.

Who Can Use It

This technology is free to any Virginia Tech student or faculty member and is a great place for graduate students to work on thesus.
